Ingredients:
 - 2 cups sugar
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 cup milk
 - 1/2 cup vegetable oil
 - 2 sugar
 - 1 cup boiling water

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 5-inch round baking
    pans.
 
 2. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour.  Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla. Mix until smooth.  Stir in
    boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.
 
 3. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until surface become little hard.
    clean. Cool completely.
